SafeHand Crisis Support System

When emotional risk looks high, MindEase does not keep the student only with AI. The SafeHand system alerts our team and connects them to a real human therapist.

1. AI risk detection

  • Detects patterns linked to self-harm or crisis language.
  • Marks the case as high-risk (without ‚Äúdiagnosing‚Äù).
  • Shows a calm, grounding message on-screen.

2. Human escalation

  • Notifies the SafeHand response team.
  • Offers a free emergency micro-session with a therapist.
  • Guides the student towards local emergency options if needed.

3. Recovery plan

  • After the free session, student gets 2 discounted follow-up sessions (5‚Äì8%).
  • Helps stabilise them instead of one-time advice.
  • Encourages longer term, ethical support.

4. Sustainability

  • Therapists are paid fairly for each session.
  • Institutions fund part of the cost through subscriptions.
  • Balances social impact with financial sustainability.

This prototype only demonstrates the concept. Real crisis features would be designed with clinical experts and clear safety protocols.

MindEase is built around the real emotional life of Indian students ‚Äì NEET attempts, JEE ranks, drop-year decisions, hostel loneliness, family pressure and relationship issues.

Unlike generic wellness apps, MindEase is:
