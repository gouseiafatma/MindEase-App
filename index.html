About MindEase

MindEase is a student-first mental health platform focused on Indian realities ‚Äì NEET attempts, JEE pressure, drop year guilt, hostel loneliness, marriage talks at home and social media comparison.

Existing apps are either too expensive or emotionally disconnected. MindEase solves this by:

  • Keeping access anonymous & affordable for students.
  • Combining AI triage with human counsellors ‚Äì not AI alone.
  • Providing institutes with early-warning dashboards without exposing any individual student.
